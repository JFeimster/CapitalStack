Marketplace FAQs

Plain-English funding questions.

Does CapitalStack guarantee approval?

No. Funding is subject to underwriting, documentation, applicant profile, product type, and funding partner criteria.

Will I see the backend funding provider?

CapitalStack presents white-labeled funding paths and routes inquiries based on fit. Backend provider details may remain internal.

What kind of businesses can use CapitalStack?

Startups, established small businesses, e-commerce sellers, real estate investors, contractors, consultants, gig workers, and operators seeking capital options.

What documents are usually needed?

Requirements vary by product. Common items include ID, bank statements, credit authorization, income verification, business financials, tax returns, invoices, or property documents.

How fast can funding happen?

Some options may move quickly when the applicant profile and documentation are strong. Others, such as real estate or structured growth loans, may take longer.

Compliance-safe disclaimer

Funding is subject to approval.
